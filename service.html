Our Services

Prodigy offers a comprehensive suite of services to meet your specific power protection needs. From initial consultation to ongoing support, we ensure your systems operate at peak performance.

UPS Installation

Our expert technicians ensure seamless installation of your UPS systems, minimizing downtime and maximizing efficiency. We handle everything from site assessment to live testing, ensuring your system is optimal from day one.

Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Our Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) provide proactive care for your UPS systems, preventing potential issues and extending their lifespan. Benefit from regular check-ups, priority support, and peace of mind.

Consultancy & System Design

Leverage our expertise to design a UPS system that perfectly aligns with your specific power requirements. Our consultants provide in-depth analysis and recommend optimal solutions.

On-Site Support

Our rapid-response on-site support team is available to address any UPS issues swiftly. We handle diagnostics, emergency repairs, and replacements, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.
